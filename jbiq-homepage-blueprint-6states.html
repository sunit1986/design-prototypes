1Header
Streak pill shame. Showing "🔥 14d" then hiding it after streak break = felt loss. May accelerate churn for ~30% of habit-app users per behavioral research.
2Value Ribbon✓ FIXED
Math is disputable. "SIP auto-optimisation ₹800" — if user claims they'd have picked that SIP anyway, the whole ribbon's credibility dies permanently.
Partner price dependency. "Saved ₹X" requires real-time price feeds from JioMart/partners. API hiccups → wrong number → trust loss.
Ribbon at ₹0 stays silent. "Start saving" nudge for 7 days before first ₹ logs may feel like empty promise.
3Updates✓ R6+P9
Boundary vs For You is fuzzy. "Fiber bill due in 3 days" — I put it in For You as alert, but Surfaces Framework says Updates is THE anchor for action-required. Blueprint self-contradicts.
Empty state feels broken. When no triggers fire for 2-3 days, users may interpret absence as system failure, not success.
4Your Assistants
12 avatars is too many. 4–5 visible, 7+ behind scroll. Casuals never discover the tail. Reorder helps power users who already know what they want.
Cold-tap Vertical failure. If any Vertical ships without a context spec, the tap loads nothing. User blames JBIQ, not the Vertical.
5For You
Signal model is out of scope. Entire ranking depends on signals that aren't in this blueprint. If signal team ships late, feed becomes random.
2P partner data freshness. "Atta ₹240 today" needs real-time inventory. Partner API hiccup = stale card = trust loss.
Content ops scale. 12 domains × 5 variants × 2 languages = thousands of copy strings per week. Team sized for this?
6Continue✓ FIXED
Progress bars will be wrong ~50%+. We can't track scroll/time across web views, JioCinema playback, external articles. "45%" may mean 100% done.
"Streak broken" copy accelerates churn. Gentle guilt triggers "fuck-it" abandonment in habit research. Needs A/B test, not a default.
Hidden until Session 6. New users miss the value of Continue; doesn't build habit early.
7Discover
"WHATS NEW" weekly cadence. Requires editorial team to ship copy every Monday for 12 domains. Without funding → recycled copy → reads as spam.
Sparkle ≠ substance. Rotating ✦ animation can feel decorative if the card itself isn't earning its slot.
8Moments✓ FIXED
Pre-written wishes feel corporate. Users won't send a JBIQ-branded canned message to family — reads as forwarded spam. Kills the viral loop.
Templates need user-editable last line to feel personal. Currently pure canned content.
WhatsApp-green CTA blurs JBIQ brand and makes the share feel like a JBIQ-sanctioned forward, not a personal wish.
9Multimodal Input
Hindi ASR quality dependency. Voice mode assumes high-quality Hindi/Hinglish recognition. Current state of Indic ASR = inconsistent for regional accents.
No visible "send" action. Pure chat input without a send arrow on empty state may confuse first-time users.
⚠Global · Cross-cutting risks
Signal model dependency. Entire home degrades without it. Assumes a signal layer that may be 6–12 months out. No degraded-mode home designed.
Content ops scale. 12 domains × card variants × languages × weekly cadence. No editorial team sized or funded for this.
CDN on raw.githubusercontent.com — fine for prototype, unacceptable for production. Needs JioCDN.
No offline / 2G story. Assets don't load on slow Bharat networks → home looks broken. Needs skeleton + cache strategy.
Motion budget undefined. FLIP + stagger + sparkle + roll-up + breath = heavy on low-end Android. Will jank on 2GB-RAM devices.
360×800 canvas dated. 2026 median device is closer to 6.5" / 400×900. Implicit device-segment decision.
No success metrics defined. Tap-through, share rate, zone-level retention — nothing. Core ↔ Vertical disputes will be unresolvable.
